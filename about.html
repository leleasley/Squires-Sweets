About Squires Sweets

Where nostalgia meets customization

Welcome to Squires Sweets - where nostalgia meets customization! We believe that everyone deserves to enjoy their favourite sweets exactly the way they want them.

Born from a passion for bringing smiles and sweetness to our community, Squires Sweets offers a unique experience - the freedom to create your own perfect sweet mix. No more settling for pre-made bags that have sweets you don't like. With us, you're in complete control!

Choose from over 40 different classic and modern sweets - from fizzy cola bottles to strawberry laces, from jelly beans to sour torpedos. Whether you're a fan of the classics or love trying new flavours, we've got something for everyone.
